The Crisis in Science Education

Outdated models, declining interest, and teacher burnout are creating a systemic challenge

Outdated Models

The 5E instructional model is over 35 years old, designed before the digital age. Teachers need modern tools that reflect today's classroom realities.

Teacher Burnout

Educators spend countless hours planning from scratch, leaving little energy for the high-touch mentoring that truly inspires students.

Declining Student Interest

STEM engagement metrics show troubling declines as traditional methods fail to connect with digital-native learners.

Our Dual-Platform Solution

AI-powered tools meet premium training to create a complete support ecosystem

The Cooper Digital Platform

An AI-augmented lesson planning system designed to work alongside teachers, not replace them.

  • 5E Model Integration: AI tools embedded within Engage, Explore, Explain, Elaborate, Evaluate framework
  • Rapid Idea Generation: Generate activities, explanations, and assessments in minutes, not hours
  • Teacher-Centered Design: Free up time for mentorship, discussion, and personalized feedback
  • Stop Planning from Scratch: Start inspiring with confidence on Monday morning

The Cooper Training Library

Premium, product-specific training that ensures teachers have both the tools and the knowledge to use them effectively.

  • Unique Unlock Model: Full training libraries unlocked when schools purchase qualifying kits through our partners

Our Story: Built in a Dojo, Born from a Mission

From personal passion to scalable platform—the journey of Cooper

The Foundation

Cooper was founded on a simple truth: great teaching requires great support. Named for a loyal friend, our company embodies partnership and commitment to educators.

The Dojo R&D Lab

Our launch is funded and informed by our founder's first venture: a traditional karate dojo. This serves as our initial funding engine, live research lab, and physical embodiment of our educational philosophy—discipline, growth, and mastery.

The Vision Realized

We're building Cooper from the ground up with authenticity, testing methods and features in a real community setting before scaling to classrooms worldwide.

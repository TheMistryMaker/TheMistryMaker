About The Builder

Hello! I'm Samir Mistry, also known as ThemistryMaker in the LEGO community. My passion for LEGO and Star Wars began as a child and has evolved into a serious artistic pursuit over the past decade.

As an active member of TOROLUG (Toronto LEGO Users Group), I participate in exhibitions, collaborative builds, and community outreach programs that bring LEGO art to the public. Our group showcases at events like the Toronto Fan Expo and local library displays.

My specialty is creating custom LEGO Star Wars models that blend film accuracy with imaginative "what-if" scenarios. From massive Imperial Star Destroyers to intricate Mos Eisley dioramas, each build tells a story and pushes the boundaries of what's possible with LEGO bricks.

When I'm not building, I'm designing new creations in Stud.io, mentoring aspiring builders, or hunting for rare LEGO pieces to complete my next project.
